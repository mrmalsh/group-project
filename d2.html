Welcome to Puerto Vallarta

Puerto Vallarta is a wonderful place that amazes and inspires everyday: a beautiful blue sky that frames a spectacular moon as well as a Sun of an incredible orange. A tempered and generous sea that relaxes, but also imposes, and sunsets that deserve a pause in our day, and take our breath away.

Los Arcos

Whether snorkeling, scubadiveing, or just looking for a great photo. Los Arcos is the place for you.

How To Get There

Driveing south out of town between the mountains and the coast is the best way to get a good look at Los Arcos from land for some of those great photo's. Seeing it from land is ok but to truly experience Los Arcos in all its glory you will need to charter a boat. Below are some options to get you up close and personal with Los Arcos.

  • Vallarta Adventures offers a scuba tour of Los Arcos here.
  • Puerto Vallarta Tours offers a snorkeling tour here.
  • If privacy, exclusivity, or doing your own thing is more up your alley Puerto Vallarta Tours also offers a range of private boat charters that can take you almost anywhere to do almost anything. Check them out here.
